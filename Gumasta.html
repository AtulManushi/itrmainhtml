The Gumasta License is a state-level registration required under the Shops and Establishment Act for businesses operating in states like Madhya Pradesh and Maharashtra. It serves as a legal permit for commercial establishments and ensures compliance with labor laws. Entrepreneurs, shop owners, service providers, and professionals with a physical office must obtain this license to operate legally.

Having a Gumasta License provides several benefits, including legal authorization, eligibility for government schemes, ease of opening a business bank account, and compliance with labor regulations. Without this license, businesses may face penalties or operational restrictions.
