Apply or Renew Trade License Online

A trade license is an official document issued by a local governing authority that allows individuals or companies to carry out specific commercial activities within a particular area. It ensures that the business complies with local laws and regulations concerning health, safety, and operational standards. Obtaining a trade license is mandatory for most types of businesses and is usually the first step before starting commercial operations.

The process of obtaining a trade license typically involves submitting an application along with relevant documents such as identity proof, business address proof, and detailed information about the business. The local municipality or licensing authority reviews the application and, if approved, grants the license for a specific period, usually one year, after which it must be renewed. Some businesses may also need additional licenses depending on the nature of their operations.
