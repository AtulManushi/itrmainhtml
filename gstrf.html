GST return filing involves reporting sales, purchases, tax collected, and tax paid to the government. Every registered business must file GST returns periodically to comply with tax regulations. Different returns apply based on the taxpayer type, such as GSTR-1 for sales, GSTR-3B for tax summaries, GSTR-4 for composition taxpayers, and GSTR-9 for annual reporting. Timely filing is crucial to avoid penalties, claim input tax credit, and maintain financial transparency.
